Coaching

Whether you are starting to learn how to fly drones, needing to pass Part 107, or being an experienced drone pilot, Levamtric is here to help. The complex nature of unmanned aviation is due it to being an emerging technology; much like personal computers were in the 1980s. Unlike personal computers, the goal of unmanned aviation is to gather aerial imagery which requires a discipline in aviation, photography, electronics, and computer science. Documentation for reference can be hard to come by and judging the quality of the content could be lacking key information to achieve an understanding. This is why it is important to have professional assistance if you value your time and efforts. Levamtric teaches through a coaching format to adapt to the needs of individuals/businesses in a 1-on-1 or group setting so that both parties can be confident that progress has been achieved towards the goal they set.
